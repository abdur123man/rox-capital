How do I open a live account?

You may open a live trading account by following the below steps:

  • Log in to your Client Portal.
  • Click the "Live Accounts" button.
  • Select your preferred account type and leverage ratio.

Confirm your choice and your account will be created automatically.

How do I fund my trading account?

You may easily fund your live trading account by following the below steps:

  • Log in to your Client Portal.
  • Navigate to "Funds" and click "Funds Transfer".
  • Select your Wallet and transfer amount.

Click "Confirm" to proceed with the funds transfer.
